## Cadence (sustainable)

1. **Weekly:** 1 social post (LinkedIn or X) pointing at **wisptools.io** or a **GitHub release**.
2. **Monthly:** 1 long-form (dev.to or industry pitch).
3. **Per major release:** GitHub Release notes + optional Show HN **or** HN (not both the same hour).

---

## What not to do

- Mass cold-email lists; duplicate posts across 10 subreddits the same day.
- Claim production guarantees you have not validated.
- Post vendor spam in technical forums without technical substance.
